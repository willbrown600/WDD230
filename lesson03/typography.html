    • The typography I have chosen for this website are Abril Fatface for the headers. I have then chosen Baloo Chettan 2 for the regular paragraphs. I chose Abril because of its serif design and to provide a different contrast against the paragraphs. I knew the paragraphs needed to be a sans-serif font to aid in the ease of reading large text without any confusion over what a certain lettering was meaning.
